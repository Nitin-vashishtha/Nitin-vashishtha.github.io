About Me

I am a solar physicist with a passion for understanding the dynamics of the solar corona and coronal mass ejections (CMEs). My research combines observational data from missions like PSP, WIND, and LASCO with advanced magnetohydrodynamic simulations using the AMRVAC framework. I specialize in forward modeling techniques, space weather impacts, and multi-wavelength diagnostics.

Research Interests

  • Numerical modeling of CME initiation and propagation
  • Forward modeling of coronagraphic and EUV observations
  • Magnetic reconnection and breakout models
  • Velocity dispersion and wave dynamics in solar plumes
  • AI/ML applications in heliophysics
